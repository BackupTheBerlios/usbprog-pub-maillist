We just etched a UsbProg board based on my schematics that are based
on your usbprog_v3_x1.brd schematics. I have a warning for you. The
actual USBN9604 device uses a 28SOIC-WIDE footprint. However, the
footprint from your .brd file is a SO28-3 (which is a 28SSOP). These
footprints are close in size, but they are not exact. You might need
to change your device's footprint to 28SOIC-WIDE.
