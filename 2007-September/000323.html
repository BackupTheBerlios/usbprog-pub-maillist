Alexey wrote on Sun, 2 Sep 2007 00:32:01 +0300
> I have changed fuses according to MCU's datasheet to enable BOD:
> BODLEVEL=0
> BODEN=0
> SUT1=0
> SUT0=0
> 
> After that I was unable to repeat any USB connection failures - they do
> not
> happen anymore. PC reliably detects usbprog whenever it becomes connected
> to
> USB port.

But there is one small problem: If you connect the target to the usbprog after you have
connected the usbprog to the usb bus, an the target gets the power from the 
programming port, the small glitch in the usb power will reset the usbprog, and the 
connection to the usb host may disappear. 

Because of this, the new usbprog 3.0 has a diode an an capacitor to stabilize the 
power supply of the mega32 on the usbprog
