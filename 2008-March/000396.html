> You need for your target a clock source. You can choose between an
> external source or you can use the internal clock source of an avr. This
> is independent from USBprog.
> 
> The only association between the USBprog clock and the target speed ist,
> that you can only progamm you target if the USBprog ISP Speed (you can
> sellect this in AVR Studio at tab board or with avrdude -B ..) is max.
> 1/4 of cpu speed.
